FINISHED!!!

Bocchi the Rock!

PROGRESS: 200% (watched in 3 days - then again in 2 weeks)

RATING: 5/5

REVIEW:
honestly, one of the better shows ive ever watched. does tell a cool story, has really nice jokes here and there. great anime if youre looking for a show with near-zero fanservice, great storytelling, some comedy and great music


Girls Band Cry

PROGRESS: 100% (watched in just 1 day)

RATING: 5/5

REVIEW:
if you dont want to watch a funny show about a band (bocchi the rock), pick this one. this anime is really great, its as if bocchi the rock had a completely different cast of characters, every one of them having a well-written backstory. one of the more emotional shows ive ever watched


Nichijou - My Ordinary Life

PROGRESS: 100% (watched in 3 days)

RATING: 4.5/5

REVIEW:
funny anime that doesnt have a strict plot line, instead its just a set of stories that try to make you laugh. some scenes really did make me laugh with all honesty. although im not giving this show 5/5 because hakase is literally the worst character imaginable yeah i get it shes just a 8-year-old kid but still shes really annoying, especially with all the changes she does to poor nano..

tho if you want a good laugh, sure, i recommend this to you


Girls' Last Tour

PROGRESS: 100% (watched 2 episodes in 1 day - rest of them after a long break)

RATING: 4/5

REVIEW:
this show is really good. dunno what to say about it really but i really liked the idea of two girls wandering around post-apocalyptic world. even though it stretches to 12 episodes, i never felt bored watching it!

i know theres probably some deep lore about the world but... i didnt really bother to dig it up
